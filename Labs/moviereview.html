The Bee Movie

The Bee Movie is a 2007 animated comedy film produced by DreamWorks Animation and directed by Simon J. Smith and Steve Hickner. The movie features the voice of Jerry Seinfeld as Barry B. Benson, a bee who has just graduated from college and is disillusioned with the prospect of working in the hive for the rest of his life.
